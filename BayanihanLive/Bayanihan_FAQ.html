Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, donations, and transparency.

How do I know my donation is safe?

We use industry-standard encryption for all transactions. Furthermore, we only partner with licensed and verified foundations. You can see public reports from these foundations on our Transparency Hub and view your own Digital Receipts.

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Currently, the BayanihanLive platform is designed to process monetary donations only. This allows our partner organizations to quickly and flexibly allocate funds to where they are most needed (e.g., for medicine, specific food items, or shelter materials) without managing logistics.

How is the "Live Map" updated?

Our live map aggregates data from multiple sources: official government alerts from PAGASA and NDRRMC, reports from our partner foundations on the ground, and verified reports from news organizations. We do not use unverified user reports to ensure accuracy.

What is the Transparency Hub?

The Transparency Hub is our commitment to Objective #2 of our project. It's a page where you can see a high-level breakdown of all funds collected and view public financial reports submitted by the foundations themselves, showing how they have used the funds received.
